My Top 5 Favourite NBA Players

  1. Chris Paul

    • 5x NBA Assists Leader (2008, 2009, 2014, 2015) 2022
    • 6x NBA Steals Leader (2008, 2009, 2011-2014)
    • 12x NBA All Star (2008-2016, 2020-2022)

    Chris Paul also known as “CP3” is one of the greatest point guards in NBA History. Standing at only 6ft tall CP3 leaves his mark on the game primarily through playmaking and operating the pick and roll. On the defensive side of the ball he is a tenacious and quick pest who is described as “stronger than he looks”. Using his high basketball IQ and quickness CP3 has led the NBA in steals multiple seasons. Over his 17 seasons in the NBA Chris Paul has estimated career earnings of $326,408,075 in salary.

  2. Jayson Tatum

    • 3x NBA All Star (2020-2022)
    • NBA All Rookie First Team (2018)
    • 2020 Tokyo Gold Medalist

    Jayson Tatum is one of the NBA’s young superstars and is regarded for his well rounded scoring ability. A 6ft 8inch small forward with a polished mid-range game, Tatum can also get to the rim. Tatum has been quoted saying he’s modeled his game after the late Kobe Bryant and this is evident from watching any Boston Celtics game. Along with other young stars like Luka Doncic and Trae Young, Tatum is expected to rise to the upper echelon of NBA players in the coming years.

  3. Goran Dragić

    • NBA All Star (2018)
    • 2017 FIBA EuroBasket Gold Medalist
    • 2017 FIBA EuroBasket MVP

    Goran Dragic, one of the minority of NBA players originating from Europe is a crafty playmaker standing at 6ft 3inches. A lefty with the fantastic footwork common in European players, Dragic can run an offense with ease while still utilizing his quickness to get to the hoop.

  4. Lebron James

    • 4x NBA Champion (2012, 2013, 2016, 2020)
    • 18x NBA All Star (2005 - 2022)
    • 2x Olympic Gold Medalist (2008, 2012)

    Lebron James, “LeGoat”, “Bron”, “The King” is commonly debated along with Micheal Jordan as the greatest basketball player to ever walk the earth. A physical specimen standing at 6ft 9inches and weighing an insane 113kg, Lebron possesses dominating strength, speed, power and leaping ability. The King’s game is centered around his all time basketball IQ, passing ability and physical gifts which allow him to be one of if not the most unstoppable drivers to the basket in NBA history. Away from the game, Lebron is an active philanthropist with multiple foundations and even his own school. Lebron is the only active NBA player in history to earn $ 1 billion.

  5. Stephen Curry

    • 3x NBA Champion (2015, 2017, 2018)
    • 2x NBA MVP (2015, 2016)
    • 50-40-90 Club (2016)

    Stephen Wardell Curry, “Chef Curry” is one of the most impactful players in NBA history and is frequently credited as the player who spearheaded the 3 point revolution with his ridiculous long range shooting. A small guard at 6ft 3inches, Currys ability to shoot with accuracy off the dribble, handle the ball and be a playmaker for others makes him one of the most unstoppable and exciting offensive weapons in history.
